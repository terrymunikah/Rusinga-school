Rusinga Schools

Rusinga is a premium private internnational school in Nairobi. With two campuses in the city, it is a co-educational, independent day and boarding school serving the international community at pre-school, primary and secondary levels. Rusinga is proud to be the leading global premium school group with operations in Europe, Asia Pacific, Africa, the Middle East and Latin America. With a network of 80 schools, across 20 countries on 5 continents, inspired schools deliver best practices from every corner of the globe to ensure learning is world class. Ruinga is also a Round Square school as well as a member of the G-30 group providing an international education to a welcoming community of 1,000 students. Rusinga has the pursuit of academic excellence at the center of its educational philosophy and approach. Rusinga fosters courage, generosity, imagination,principle and resolve so that children emerge empowered with the skill and abilities to be the leaders and guardians of tomorrow's world.
